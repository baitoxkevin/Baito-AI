## 3. 🎯 3D CARD STACK with FLIP ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Inspired by**: Apple Card UI, Modern card decks

**Visual Example**:
```
                 [All Cards Stacked]

        ┌──────────────────┐
       ┌┼──────────────────┼┐
      ┌┼┼──────────────────┼┼┐
     ┌┼┼┼──────────────────┼┼┼┐
     │││     [Photo]       │││
     │││                   │││
     │││    John Doe       │││
     │││    ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐     │││
     │││    Age: 28        │││
     └┴┴───────[Hover to  ┴┴┴┘
               Flip]

            [On Hover - Flips 3D]

     ╔════════════════════╗
     ║   BACK SIDE        ║
     ║                    ║
     ║ 📞 +6012-345-6789 ║
     ║ 📧 john@email.com ║
     ║                    ║
     ║ Skills:            ║
     ║ • Event Setup      ║
     ║ • Crowd Control    ║
     ║ • Customer Service ║
     ║                    ║
     ║ Projects: 15       ║
     ║ On-time: 98%       ║
     ╚════════════════════╝
```

**Interaction**:
- Cards stack like physical cards
- Hover = Flip to back (3D rotation)
- Click = Pull card to front
- Swipe away = Next card
- Beautiful depth effects

**Why Cool**:
- Feels like physical cards
- Smooth 3D animations
- Front = Quick info
- Back = Details
- Very engaging
